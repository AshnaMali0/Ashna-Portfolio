Digital Taxidermy

2025

Created with Isadora and Adobe Aftereffects

Digital Taxidermy is an installation created by John Gu, Sinclair Li and myself. The installation consists of videos projection-mapped onto cardboard monitors, which the audiences can interact with using a remote.

The installation explores a future where pigeons have gone extinct. Based on user interaction, it switches from digital footage to humanistic stories of pigeons.

Digital Taxidermy was displayed at the Live Media Processing course end-of-year show.
