Are You Afraid to Think about It?

2024

Created with Fusion

Are You Afraid to Think about It? was created in a fabrication course. The design was created in Fusion and CNC'ed onto a wood block. It was photographed in Madison Square Park, New York.

Are You Afraid to Think about It? was displayed in ITP/IMA's Spring Show 2024, where audience members were encouraged to leave a note with the fear that first came to mind.
