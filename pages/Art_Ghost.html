Art Ghost

2025

Created with Fusion and Adobe Illustrator

Art Ghost is an installation created by Link Wang and myself that consists of a mechanical hand moving behind a canvas.

To create it, we used linear rail to build a core-xy pulley system that was 6 feet wide and 5 feet tall. Link modelled and engineered the custom 3D parts that would work with the system. I put together the electronics, which include the motors, wiring and coding, to move the hand. In the end, the machine was able to trace patterns created in Adobe Illustrator.

Art Ghost was displayed at ITP/IMA's 2025 Spring Show.
